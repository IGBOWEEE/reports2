Progressive Accumulation

The first six months of Intmax on the Ethereum Mainnet tell a story of steady, organic growth rather than volatile speculation. Unlike many Layer 2 launches that see an immediate crash after an initial hype spike, Intmax has demonstrated a consistent month-over-month ramp-up.

The protocol has processed a cumulative total of nearly $60 Million in volume since launch. Crucially, user activity hit an all-time high in Q4 2025, driven by tangible product announcements like the Japanese JPYC stablecoin and privacy tech reveals at Devconnect.
